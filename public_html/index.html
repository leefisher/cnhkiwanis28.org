Kiwanis is a worldwide organization of volunteers dedicated to improving the world, one child and one community at a time. We have been around for over 100 years and are in 80 countries, serving with pride.

There are currently eight Kiwanis Clubs in southern Nevada, all providing dedicated service to the communities we love. Whether you are one of our fantastic members or looking for a way to give back to your community this is the place to be! Take a look at our calendar and you will see just how busy we are. If you see something that sounds like fun you are welcome to join us.
