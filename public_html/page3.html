LAS VEGAS YOUNG PROFESSIONALS

This club is part of a new style of service club that caters to adults who are developing their careers, recent college graduates and those who are still young at heart. Their goal is to develop young adults into community leaders.

CENTENNIAL HILLS

One of our newest clubs, chartered in January, 2018. They are making their presence known in the northwest area of southern Nevada

GREEN VALLEY

Celebrating 30 years of service in Henderson, the members are extremely active, sponsoring seven Key Clubs, a Builder Club and a Circle K Club. They are also involved in supporting The Salvation Army, Serving Our Kids and Easter Seals.

LAS VEGAS

Our oldest club with 94 years serving Southern Nevada. They are very successful with their Varsity Quiz program, Kiwanis/Rotary Thanksgiving Food Drive and support of the Springs Preserve. They are a font of local history and knowledge.

LAS VEGAS STRIP

Weighing in with 46 years of volunteer service they are a force to be reckoned with. A very strong club, lots of projects and service.

NORTH LAS VEGAS

Small yet mighty, this 13 year old club provides much needed service to the North Las Vegas area.

PAHRUMP VALLEY

Over the hump in Pahrump our 23 year old club is strong and they serve from the heart.

SOUTHWEST LAS VEGAS

Another of our new clubs from January, 2018, they are already on the road to becoming a vital part of the Las Vegas Valley.
