For Kiwanis International, partnerships are an exciting opportunity - and the right
 partnerships are paramount. We welcome corporate partnerships that align with
 our mission and preserve the trust of our members and the communities, children
 and families we serve. Our partners’ products, services and reputation must:
●
Be compatible with and complementary to Kiwanis’ mission and values.
●
Reflect a high degree of intergrity.
●
Demonstrate a track record of high quality products or services.

 Our partners bring value to clubs by providing services and products that enhance
 service and work in the community. There are multiple levels of partnerships, each
 with a unique program or product.
